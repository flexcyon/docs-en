## Configuration Options

### Graph View Controls Vertical Padding

CSS目标变量:`var(--graph-control-verti-padding)`
默认: 6 (px)

### Graph View Controls Horizontal Padding

CSS目标变量:`var(--graph-control-horiz-padding)`
默认: 12 (px)
