---
title: "2.0 设置迁移器"
---

您可以使用我们的客户端迁移工具来更新您的样式设置
从 2.0.0 之前到 2.0.0 的无缝配置. 它只修改目标
flexcyon 样式设置 JSON 键值对, 并按字母顺序对其进行排序.
其他 JSON 键值对不会被修改.

> 这需要使用 Web Assembly (wasm), 确保您的浏览器支持
wasm.
>
> 参见 [caniuse](https://caniuse.com/wasm) 关于兼容性, 如果你在
一个较旧的浏览器, 尽管如此 wasm 通常应该是兼容的.

## 用法

1. 在 Obsidian 中, 打开 `Settings` (设置), 然后单击 `Community Plugins`
(社区插件) 下的 `Style Settings` (样式设置).

2. 单击 `Export` (导出) 和 `Copy to clipboard` (复制到剪贴板). 
然后关闭模态. 保持 `Style Settings` (样式设置) 视图打开.

3. Paste from your clipboard to the migration tool input below.

> Alternatively, you can replace step 2 and 3 by uploading `data.json`
> located at:
>
> `~/path_to_your_vault/.obsidian/plugins/obsidian-style-settings/`

**Remember to backup your Style Settings before proceeding.**

4. Switch window focus back to Obsidian.

5. Click `Import` and paste output of migration tool from system clipboard.

6. Click `Save` and you have just migrated your Style Settings to version
2.0's format.

> Alternatively, you can replace step 4 to 6 by downloading `migrated_settings.json`
> and writing (in place) to `data.json` in place, located at:
>
> `~/path_to_your_vault/.obsidian/plugins/obsidian-style-settings/`
