Callout / Callout 元数据:

- "title-opacity-`<opacity-value>`"

- "content-opacity-`<opacity-value>`"

- "all-opacity-`<opacity-value>`"

用法:

```md
> [!info|title-opacity-0.5 content-opacity-0.8] The callout title will have an opacity of 0.5
> The callout content will have an opacity of 0.8
```

```md
> [!info|all-opacity-0.55] The callout title will have an opacity of 0.55
> The callout content will have an opacity of 0.5
```
