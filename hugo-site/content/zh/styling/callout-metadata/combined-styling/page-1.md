Callout 元数据: "empty"

用法:

```md
> [!|empty] Neither the title and callout icon will show
> Content is shown as usual
```

或者, 您可以使用:

```md
> [!info|empty] Neither the title and callout icon will show
> Content is shown as usual
```
> ["no-icon"](../icon-styling/page-1) 和 ["no-title"](../title-styling/page-1) 的简写
