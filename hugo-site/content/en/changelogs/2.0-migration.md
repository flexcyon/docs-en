title: "Settings Migrator"
---

You can use our client-side tool to update your Style Settings
configurations from pre-2.0.0 to 2.0.0 seamlessly.

> This requires the use of Web Assembly (wasm), ensure your browser supports 
wasm. See [caniuse](https://caniuse.com/wasm) on compatibility if you are on
an older browser, though wasm should be generally compatible.

## Usage

1. Go to `Settings`, then click on `Style Settings` under `Community Plugins`.
2. Click `Export` and `Copy to clipboard`. Then close the modal. Leave
the Style Settings view open.
3. Paste from your clipboard to the migration tool input below.
4. Switch window focus back to Obsidian.
5. Click `Import` and paste output of migration tool from system clipboard.
6. Click `Save` and you have just migrated your Style Settings to version
2.0's format.
