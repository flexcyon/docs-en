---
title: Capitalize Content
---

Callout metadata: "caps-content"/"capitalize-content"

Usage:

```md
> [!info|caps-content] The title will be displayed as usual
> The content will be displayed as caps

> [!info|capitalize-content] The title will be displayed as usual
> The content will be displayed as caps
```

The style itself is also applied to ["caps-all"](../combined-styling/page-16)
along with ["caps-title"](../title-styling/page-16).
