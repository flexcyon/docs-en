### Fonts

- [Cascadia Code](https://github.com/microsoft/cascadia-code) is a fun, new monospaced font
that includes programming ligatures and is designed to enhance the modern look and feel
of the Windows Terminal.  

It is licensed under the [SIL Open Font License](https://openfontlicense.org)

- [Departure Mono](https://departuremono.com) is a monospaced pixel font by Helena Zhang.

It is licensed under the [SIL Open Font License](https://openfontlicense.org)

- [Maple Mono](https://github.com/subframe7536/Maple-font) is an open source monospace font
with round corner, ligatures and Nerd-Font icons for IDE and terminal, fine-grained
customization options.

Its characteristic of perfect 2:1 alignment between Chinese and English allows the font to
achieve a neat, uniform, beautiful, and comfortable appearance in scenarios such as multilingual
display and Markdown tables.

It is licensed under the [SIL Open Font License](https://openfontlicense.org)
