Callout 元数据: "rtl-content"

用法:

```md
> [!info|rtl-content] The title will be displayed as usual
> The content will be displayed as rtl
```

这种风格本身也适用于 ["rtl-all"](../combined-styling/page-11.md) 和 ["rtl-title"](../title-styling/page-11.md)。
